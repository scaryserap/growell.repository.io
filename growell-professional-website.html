About

Built for laboratories that need reliable sourcing.

Growell Instruments serves research institutions, industrial laboratories, colleges, diagnostics environments, and pharmaceutical buyers with a broad scientific equipment portfolio.

What we supply

Our range includes incubators, autoclaves, water baths, mixers, analytical instruments, microscopes, spectrophotometers, HPLC support products, fume hoods, laminar air flow systems, and other core lab equipment.

Growell brand support

Heating and cooling equipment are supplied under the Growell brand, with installation and demonstration support. This helps customers source from one partner while reducing setup friction.
